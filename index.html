On touch: tap the center to play or pause, tap the left or right edge to jump a sentence, hold the word to save the sentence to notes, swipe for sentence jumps.

Pause any time you feel lost: the full sentence appears with your place marked. Position is saved automatically.

iPad: open this site in Safari, tap Share, then Add to Home Screen. It becomes a full-screen app and works offline. Turn on sync in settings to carry your library and positions between devices.
